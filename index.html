001 / Profile

Keenan Oliver is a South African interdisciplinary designer and cultural producer. His practice operates as a cultural engine for world-building, working across design, publishing, and collaborative projects to explore the intersections of art, design, fashion, architecture, film, and object-making.

Through visual systems, editorial design, and creative direction, he produces work that both shapes and documents contemporary creative practice. Alongside client and institutional collaborations, his work extends into publications, exhibitions, and cultural programming, forming an evolving archive of contemporary culture.

Rooted in South Africa and engaged globally, his practice connects disciplines, collaborators, and contexts, positioning design as both a tool for production and a method of cultural documentation.
